What is GOMYTEACHER?

GOMYTEACHER is an AI-powered platform designed to help teachers streamline their work, provide real-time student feedback, and create personalized learning content.

  • Automated Grading & Feedback – AI evaluates assignments, quizzes, and essays with high accuracy
  • AI-Generated Lesson Plans & Exercises – Save time with customized course content
  • Adaptive Learning for Students – AI adjusts learning material based on student progress
  • Seamless LMS Integration – Works with Google Classroom, Moodle, and Blackboard
